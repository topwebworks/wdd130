Overview

Purpose

Market river rafting tours to people interested in outdoor activities. Phase 2 could be upsell outdoor gear bundles - tested and shown by the rafting guides.

Audience

Targeted towards casual and expert outdoor enthusiasts. From casual lazy river to white water. Clients look for quality outdoor sports activity and gear. And understand being around expert guides in the field can save their life. This demographic does not mind spending what it takes to keep them safe while having an great time outdoors.
